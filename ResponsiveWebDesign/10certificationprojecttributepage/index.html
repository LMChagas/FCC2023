DJ Premier

Greatest Hip Hop producer ever

Here are some highlights of DJ Premier career

  • 1966 - Christopher Edward Martin was born in the Fifth Ward of Houston, Texas on March 21, 1966
  • 1989 - Moves to brooklyn,NY at the behest of GURU to join his group, GANG STARR. In the same year they release theyr first album No More Mr. Nice Guy
  • 1991 - GANG STARR releases the album Step in the Arena with standout tracks, "Just to Get a Rep" and "Who's Gonna Take the Weight?"
  • 1992 - GANG STARR releases the album Daily Operation with standout tracks, "Ex Girl to Next Girl" and "I'm the Man"
  • 1994 - A banner year for DJ PREMIER with the release of the GANG STARR album Hard to Earn that had the classic song "Mass Appeal". And classic records like NAS-"N.Y. State of Mind", JERU THE DAMAJA-"Come Clean", THE NOTORIOUS BIG-"Unbelievable"
  • 1995-1997 - Highlights from this years include: KRS-ONE-"MC's Act Like They Don't Know", JAY-Z-"D'Evils", NAS-"I gave you power", THE NOTORIOUS BIG-"ten crack commandments"
  • 1998 - Was the year GANG STARR released theyr classic album Moment of truth
  • 1999-2013 - Years of continued excelence with classic production like: NAS-"nas is like", MOSDEF-"mathematics", D'ANGELO-"devil's pie", DEVIN THE DUDE-"Doobie Ashtray", ROYCE DA 5'9"-"Boom" and many others
  • 2014-2023 -After the passing of his GANGSTARR partner GURU, PREMIER collaborated with ROYCE DA 5'9" for two critically aclaimed albums as PRYME
